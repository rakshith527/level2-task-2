Introduction

Punith Rajkumar is a popular actor and singer from Karnataka, India. He has starred in many successful movies and has won several awards for his performances. Rajkumar is also known for his philanthropic work and has been actively involved in various charitable organizations.

  • Jackie
  • Milana
  • Rajakumara
  • Yuvarathnaa
